Home

This website represents a structured academic research journey aimed at explaining my decision to study in Turkey. It highlights the academic, cultural, and practical factors that guided my choice of country, cities, and universities, with a clear focus on long-term educational and career planning.

The content is based on analysis, comparison, and preparation, not personal travel experiences, and reflects my commitment to making an informed and realistic academic decision.

Why Turkey

Turkey represents a strong academic destination that combines quality higher education with cultural familiarity and strategic geographical positioning. Its universities offer well-structured engineering programs, modern academic facilities, and increasing international recognition, especially in technical fields such as Computer Engineering.

From a cultural perspective, Turkey provides a social environment compatible with Middle Eastern values, while remaining modern and internationally connected.

Cities I Chose

Sakarya

Sakarya was chosen for its calm atmosphere and strong connection to nature, making it highly suitable for focused student life. The city offers a simple and organized daily routine, mild weather, and a peaceful environment that supports academic discipline.

Konya

Konya stands out for its disciplined and organized lifestyle, which directly supports academic seriousness. The presence of a large student community within a calm environment encourages long-term academic growth.

Denizli

Denizli offers a balanced environment that is neither overcrowded nor limited. Its relaxed atmosphere helps reduce academic stress while maintaining essential student services.

Samsun

Samsun is a lively coastal city that combines academic suitability with social and sports activities, without the pressure of very large metropolitan cities.

Mersin

Mersin offers a Mediterranean atmosphere that supports a positive student lifestyle and a healthy balance between academics and personal life.

Universities I Chose

Sakarya University

Sakarya University offers a large and organized academic environment with strong facilities such as libraries, laboratories, and student spaces. Exchange programs and student clubs support both academic and personal growth.

Selçuk University

Selçuk University provides a strong academic tradition and a Computer Engineering program focused on modern technologies, system design, and practical skills.

Ondokuz Mayıs University

Ondokuz Mayıs University offers solid infrastructure in engineering fields, emphasizing both theoretical knowledge and practical applications.

Pamukkale University

Pamukkale University provides a supportive academic environment focused on practical education and long-term technical development.

Mersin University

Mersin University offers a balanced academic structure with strong foundations in computer science and applied learning opportunities.

Cultural Similarities

Turkey and Syria share many cultural and social similarities, including strong family values, social traditions, and daily lifestyle habits. These similarities create a familiar environment for Syrian and Arab students.

The Turkish language is relatively close to Arabic, which helps Arab students integrate faster and communicate more confidently in daily life.
